Sparklines is an upcoming thesis show by Doug MacDowell (me). This show is a culmination of my Master of Fine Arts degree at Ohio University. The show visualizes data portraits and landscapes, positioned within the lineage of coffee maker computers. The work is multi media and will feature two-dimensional and three-dimensional artwork.

I am sharing the space with my colleague Chad Robert Collofello whose show name is Reawaken. Chad's show is profoundly personal and inspired by his journey to rediscover birdsong, a childhood fascination he lost due to hearing impairment and urban noise.

The reception for this show is Friday, April 18, 2025 from 6-8PM at Majestic Galleries in Nelsonville, Ohio. The address is: 20 Public Square, Nelsonville, OH 45764

I anticipate the reception to be a blast and I happily invite you to join us!

Nelsonville is a rural town and Majestic Galleries is an elegant and large space. I anticipate to provide food. Because this is a university event, we cannot provide alcohol. But there are bars adjacent to the gallery - I recommend Rhapsody. Athens is also a skip away. I recommend Jackie's Taproom or Little Fish Brewing.

My and Chad's show is open for a total of three days (April 18-20, 2025). For those of you who live far away and have received my postcard or message, please know I will be documenting the artwork and sharing on my website.
