Title: Percolating Human Computer Interaction

Year: 2024

Medium: GE Coffeematic Coffee Maker 10 Cup, ASUS M2NPV-VM Motherboard, 8GB DDR2 RAM, Linux Mint OS, HIS HD 4670 IceQ Graphics Card, Starbucks Pike Place blend

Dimension: 2 x 1.5 x 1.5 feet


Percolating HCI is an ongoing project where a coffee gadget is interfaced with DDR3 unbuffered DIMMS.

The coffeemaker/computer examines flows from perspectives of percolation and design.

I hope this project extends headroom within the operating environment while percolating the melodies encountered in its configuration.
