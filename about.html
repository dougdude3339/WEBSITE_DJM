Doug MacDowell

is a data analyst, maker, and collector of George Foreman Grills.

Doug resides in central Ohio and holds an MFA in Studio Art and BA in Sociology. He has worked professionally as a Data Analyst for over 9 years. He has shown artwork in two solo exhibitions and many group exhibitions. He has co-authored 7 peer-reviewed research articles along with an array of other work related to research, arts, and teaching.
